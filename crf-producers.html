What to expect

As a CRF producer

Step 1/6

Let’s Get Acquainted First!

Discuss your crop applications, CRF strategy and partnership goals with the Triferto team.

You Define The CRF Formulation

Select the CRF formulation you wish to use in NutriBag™. Flexible dosage options ranging from 10 to 50 grams per bag are available. Our team is happy to support you.

Rootzone Qualification

Then we validate whether the CRF formulation is suitable and safe for direct rootzone placement based on NutriBag™ guidelines.

Production by Triferto

Using our specialized toll bagging technology in Zwijnaarde, we package the selected and pre-calibrated fertilizer formulation into the NutriBags, ensuring consistent quality and accuracy.

Packaging & Branding

Explore toll bagging, private label, co-branding or NutriBag™ Rootzone branding possibilities. Available in multiple packaging formats: 10 kg bags, and 1.2 tonne big bags.
