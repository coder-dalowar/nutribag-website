NutriBag™ Rootzone

Bring smarter nutrient delivery to horticulture and specialty crop markets, under our label or yours. Small in form. Significant in impact. Designed for accurate rootzone placement and operational simplicity.

Explore NutriBag™ Explore Partnership Options

The New Solution

The fertilizer market is changing: Increasing environmental regulations, pressure on nutrient efficiency and the demand for more sustainable crop production. With NutriBag™, Triferto introduces a unique and next-generation fertilizer application method designed to support more efficient nutrient use with lower fertilizer input.

Our goal is to help partners bring innovative, future-focused fertilizer solutions to the market through flexible partnership models, including private label and toll bagging options.

More About Triferto

How It Works

NutriBag™ Rootzone combines selected CRF nutrition, pre-calibrated dosage and a 100% biodegradable fertilizer bag, designed for simple and accurate placement directly in the rootzone.

Suitable for professional growing markets:

Vegetables Success stories Soft fruit Success stories Orchards Success stories Vineyards Success stories Nurseries Success stories Forestry Success stories

Why It Matters

NutriBag™ Rootzone brings the 4R Stewardship principles together in one practical CRF concept. It helps partners offer a simple, market-ready solution built around a custom fertilizer blend, precise dosage, controlled release and direct rootzone placement.

Right source
Custom Fertilizer Blend

Fertilizer compositions are tailored to crop-specific nutritional requirements.

Right rate
Precise Dosage

Pre-calibrated dosing supports accurate nutrient application while helping reduce nutrient losses and over-application.

Right place
Rootzone Placement

Nutrients are positioned directly in the rootzone to support efficient uptake and targeted application.

Right time
Controlled Release

CRF technology gradually releases nutrients over time based on crop requirements.

You Define The CRF Formulation

Select the CRF formulation you wish to use in NutriBag™. Flexible dosage options ranging from 10 to 50 grams per bag are available. Our team is happy to support you.

Rootzone Qualification

Then we validate whether the CRF formulation is suitable and safe for direct rootzone placement based on NutriBag™ guidelines.
